 Kitchen Experiments

Since I no longer work in a science lab, my kitchen has become my venue for experimentation. I'm especially interested in recipes that challenge assumptions with their simplicity (i.e. pea soup = pea + water + salt) or their unusual use of ingredients (e.g. muffin = almond flour + egg).
